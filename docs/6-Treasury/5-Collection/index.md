<b>Collection</b>
===

<br />
<br />
<br />

<b>Mode of Operation</b>
---

<br />
<br />

### <b>1. ONLINE</b>

Generally intended to be used as tellering. The transaction date is automatically assigned by the system and the record is not editable once committed. In cases of errors, the receipt must be voided.

<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Issue Cash Receipt</b> on the <b>Collection</b> sub-menu.
<br />

![alt text][cash receipt]

<br />
<br />

b.) Select the type of transaction: Mode (should be ONLINE), AF Type and Collection Type. Then click <b>Next</b>.
<br />

![alt text][type of transaction]

<br />
<br />

c.) Fill up the payer info. Click the <b>lookup</b> icon on the <b>Payer</b> text field to view the list of all the entities registered in the system. Or simply type the taxpayer's name on the payer text field and press Enter key.
<br />

![alt text][payer info]

<br />
<br />

d.) Select the entity and click ok.
<br />

![alt text][select entity]

<br />
<br />

e.) After selecting an entity, the payer info is automatically filled up derived from the information of the Taxpayer Entities. To edit or update the entity info, click the <b>Open</b> icon on the payer text field and the entity window will pop up.

![alt text][update entity]

<br />
<br />

f.) If payer's personal information is not yet registered in the system, click the <b>
 Document</b> icon on the payer text field and the user will be redirected to Individual Entity (Create) window to continue creating new entity.

![alt text][create entity]
 
<br />
<br />

g.) Click the <b>Search</b> icon on the Account Title text field to open the lookup list of all the accountable forms registered in the system. Type the account title on the search bar and press Enter key. 

![alt text][search af]
 
<br />
<br />

h.) Select the accountable form and click OK. Enter the quantity and click OK.

![alt text][enter qty]
 
<br />
<br />

i.) The amount is automatically filled up derived from the data in the system. Press Enter key to commit.

![alt text][commit af]

<br />
<br />

j.) Payment Methods are <b>Cash, Check and Electronic Fund Transfer</b>.

![alt text][payment methods]

<br />
<br />

l.) The system calculates the cash change. Prepare the cash change and click OK.

![alt text][cash change]

<br />
<br />

m.) Ensure that the following receipt number matches the actual receipt to use. Otherwise, do not proceed. Press Enter key or click OK to confirm. The print dialog box will pop up to print the receipt.

![alt text][confirm receipt]

<br />
<br />

n.) The transaction details and options are shown on the image below:

![alt text][transaction details]

- Quit - to exit the online collection.
- Add Another - to add another cash receipt transaction.
- Reprint - to print again the cash receipt. It requires a remark before reprinting.
- Print Detail - to print the details of the cash receipt without following the receipt format.
- Void - to revoke the cash receipt transaction. It requires the username, password and remarks of the approver.
- Collection Summary - ?????

<br />
<br />

### <b>2. OFFLINE</b>

This mode can be used as tellering or capturing of previous transactions. The main difference between offline and online mode is that any information can be modified even if the record is already committed. This is designed to be used in cases such as power interruption, hardware failures, etc.

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Issue Cash Receipt</b> on the <b>Collection</b> sub-menu.
<br />

![alt text][cash receipt]

<br />
<br />

b.) Select the type of transaction: Mode (should be OFFLINE), AF Type and Collection Type. Fill up the <b>Official Receipt Date</b> and click <b>Next</b>.

![alt text][offline mode]

 Document</b> icon on the payer text field and the user will be redirected to Individual Entity (Create) window to continue creating new entity.

### <b>3. CAPTURE/FIELD</b>

This mode can be used to capture field collections. It allows full editing capability in order to simplify correction data for counter-checking and ensuring correctness. Once posted, capture data can no longer be edited.

<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>View Batch Captures</b> on <b>Collection</b> sub-menu.

<br />

![alt text][view batch captures]

<br />
<br />

b.) Click the <b>New</b> icon to issue batch capture.

<br />

![alt text][new batch capture]

<br />
<br />

c.) The user will be redirected to <b>New Batch Cash Receipt</b> tab. Input the <b>Start Series</b> based on the actual receipt used during field collection and click <b>Next</b>. Select the stub from the lookup list and click OK to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][start series]

<br />
<br />

d.) Click <b>Add</b> to start capturing the batch cash receipt.

<br />

![alt text][add batch capture]

<br />
<br />

e.) Fill up the General Information based on the actual cash receipt. Click the <b>Search</b> icon on the Account Title text field to open the lookup list of all the accountable forms registered in the system. Type the account title on the search bar and press Enter key. If the payment was made through check, click the <b>Check</b> tab and click <b>Add</b>. Enter the check details and click <b>Save and Continue</b>. Then click <b>Save</b> to proceed to the next transaction.

<br />

![alt text][batch capture]

![alt text][add check]

<br />
<br />

f.) After capturing all the cash receipts, click <b>Submit For Posting</b>. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][submit for posting]

<br />
<br />
 
g.) If all the cash receipts captured are correct, click <b>Post</b>. If there is an incorrect entry of the cash receipts, click <b>Disapprove</b> to go back to the previous page.

<br />

![alt text][post batch capture]

<br />
<br />

h.) Click <b>Submit For Online Remittance</b> to remit all cash and checks received to the liquidating officer. If there is an incorrect entry, click <b>Revert</b> to go back to the previous page.

<br />

![alt text][submit for online remittance]

<br />
<br />

i.) Click <b>Close</b> to exit Batch Capture Collection.

<br />

![alt text][close batch capture]
