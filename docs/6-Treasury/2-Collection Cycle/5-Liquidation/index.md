<b>5. Liquidation of Collections</b>
---

<br />

<b>Job Title: LIQUIDATING OFFICER</b>

<b>Required Role: TREASURY.LIQUIDATING_OFFICER</b>

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Prepare Collection Voucher</b> on the <b>Remittance/Deposit</b> sub-menu.

<br />

![alt text][prepare collection voucher]

<br />
<br />

b.) Select and double-click the collection remitted by the collector to view the report.

<br />

![alt text][liq view report]

<br />
<br />

c.) If the Report on Collections and Deposits is correct, click <b>Accept</b>. Otherwise, click <b>Send Back</b> to send back the remittance to the collector.

<br />

![alt text][accept remittance]

<br />
<br />

d.) Click <b>Affix Collector Signature</b> and <b>Affix Approver Signature</b> to attach the signatures on the report. Then click <b>Close</b> to exit.

<br />

![alt text][affix signature]

<br />
<br />

e.) Click <b>Submit for Liquidation</b>. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][submit liq]

<br />
<br />

f.) Click <b>Update Cash Breakdown</b> and input all the cash received by each currency denomination.

<br />

![alt text][update cash breakdown]

<br />
<br />

g.) Click <b>Post</b>. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][post]

<br />
<br />

h.) Various liquidation reports are automatically generated under <b>Reports</b> button.

<br />

![alt text][liquidation report]

<br />
<br />
<br />
