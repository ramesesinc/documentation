<b>Remittance of Cash and Checks</b>
---

<br />

<b>Job Title: COLLECTOR</b>

<b>Required Role: TREASURY.COLLECTOR</b>

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Prepare Remittance (Remit)</b> on the <b>Collection</b> sub-menu.

<br />

![alt text][remit]

<br />
<br />

b.) The receipts summary and voided receipts are displayed. Click <b>Submit for Remittance</b>. A message will pop up confirming the remittance is being submitted. Press Enter key or click OK to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][submit remittance]

<br />
<br />

c.) Click <b>Cash Breakdown</b> to input all the cash received by each currency denomination.

<br />

![alt text][cash breakdown]

<br />
<br />

d.) Enter the quantity of each currency denomination.

*Note: "Cash Remaining" should be "0.00 (zero)".*

<br />

![alt text][denomination]

<br />
<br />

e.) After assessing all the cash and checks received, click <b>Submit for Liquidation</b> to forward the remittance to the liquidation officer. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][submit liquidation]

<br />
<br />

f.) Click <b>Change Liquidating Officer</b> to choose a liquidating officer who will liquidate the remittance submitted. Select a liquidating officer from the lookup list and click OK to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][change liquidating officer]

<br />
<br />

g.) Various remittance reports are automatically generated under <b>Reports</b> button.

<br />

![alt text][remittance reports]

<br />
<br />
<br />
