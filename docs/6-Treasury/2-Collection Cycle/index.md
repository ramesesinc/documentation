<b>Collection Process Flow</b>
===

<br />

![alt text][collection process]

<br />

The collection process follows the standard government operation guidelines. The following describes the typical cycle for an online collection:

<br />

1. The Collector requests an accountable form in the system through an online request (RIS). The RIS is forwarded to the Acountable Form Officer (AFO).

2. The Accountable Form Officer issues the accountable form requested by the Collector.

3. The Collector receives the actual stub with a receipt form from the AFO. The Collector can now perform online collection through the terminal and print real-time receipt.

4. The Collector performs a remittance transaction in the system for all the cash and checks received within that day. The remittance form is printed and the cash and checks are forwarded to the liquidating officer.

5. The Liquidating Officer receives all the collections from the different collectors and prepares a consolidated report to be forwarded to the cashier. Reports are also segregated by fund.

6. The Cashier receives the liquidation report from the liquidating officers. The cashier prepares the deposit slip from the system and posts the deposit once confirmed.

7. Revenue reports are automatically generated, e.g., SRE and NGAS.

### <b>1. Requisition of Accountable Form</b>

<br />

<b>Job Title: COLLECTOR</b>

<b>Required Role: TREASURY.COLLECTOR</b>

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Request Accountable Form</b> on the <b>Collection</b> sub-menu.
<br />

![alt text][request af]

<br />
<br />

b.) Click the <b>Search</b> icon under the item code to open the lookup list of the accountable forms. Select an Accountable Form and click OK. Or simply type the item code on the text field and click OK. The <b>title</b> and <b>unit</b> will automatically be filled up. Input the number of stub under <b>Qty</b> and press Enter key to commit.
<br />

![alt text][item code]

<br />
<br />

c.) Click the <b>Save</b> icon. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][save request af]

<br />
<br />

d.) The requisition of accountable form is successful with a "Req. No" for reference. Click the <b>Preview</b> icon to view the "Requisition and Issue Slip" report.

<br />

![alt text][view ris]

<br />
<br />

e.) Click the <b>Save</b> icon to save a document file of the RIS report. Click the <b>Print</b> icon to print the RIS report.

<br />

![alt text][ris report]

<br />
<br />
<br />

### <b>2. Issuance of Accountable Form</b>

<br />

<b>Job Title: ACCOUNTABLE FORM OFFICER / AFO</b>

<b>Required Role: TREASURY.AFO</b>

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>New AF Transaction</b> on the <b>Accountable Forms</b> sub-menu.

<br />

![alt text][new af transaction]

<br />
<br />

b.) Select <b>Issue</b> as Type of Transaction.

<br />

![alt text][issue]

<br />
<br />

c.) Select <b>With Request</b> and click the <b>Search</b> icon to open the lookup request list.

<br />

![alt text][with request]

<br />
<br />

d.) Select the RIS and click OK.

<br />

![alt text][lookup ris]

<br />
<br />

e.) Click <b>Next</b> to proceed to the draft mode of the AF transaction.

<br />

![alt text][issue next]

<br />
<br />

f.) Edit and verify the Date, Collector and Particulars. Click <b>Next</b>. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][issue proceed]

<br />
<br />

g.) Click <b>Issue</b>, input the quantity to issue, and click OK.

<br />

![alt text][issue af]

<br />

![alt text][issue qty]

<br />
<br />

h.) If there is an incorrect entry of issuance, use <b>Revert</b> to go back on the issuance of accountable form. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][revert]

<br />
<br />

![alt text][revert yes]

<br />
<br />

i.) After verifying the data, click <b>Post</b> to post the AF transaction to the system. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][post af]

<br />
<br />

j. The AF transaction is posted in the system. (Refer to the image below) Click <b>Preview</b> to view, print or save the Requisition and Issue Slip report.

<br />

![alt text][preview af]

<br />
<br />

![alt text][ris]

### <b>3. Collection of Cash Receipt</b>

<br />

<b>Job Title: COLLECTOR</b>

<b>Required Role: TREASURY.COLLECTOR</b>

<br />
<br />

I.) <b>ONLINE</b> - generally intended to be used as tellering. The transaction date is automatically assigned by the system and the record is not editable once committed. In case of errors, the receipt must be voided.

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Issue Cash Receipt</b> on the <b>Collection</b> sub-menu.
<br />

![alt text][cash receipt]

<br />
<br />

b.) Select the type of transaction: Mode (should be ONLINE), AF Type and Collection Type. Then click <b>Next</b>.
<br />

![alt text][type of transaction]

<br />
<br />

c.) Fill up the payer info. Click the <b>lookup</b> icon on the <b>Payer</b> text field to view the list of all the entities registered in the system. Or simply type the taxpayer's name on the payer text field and press Enter key.
<br />

![alt text][payer info]

<br />
<br />

d.) Select the entity and click ok.
<br />

![alt text][select entity]

<br />
<br />

e.) After selecting an entity, the payer info is automatically filled up derived from the information from the Taxpayer Entities. To edit or update the entity info, click the <b>Open</b> icon on the payer text field and the entity window will pop up.

![alt text][update entity]

<br />
<br />

f.) If payer's personal information is not yet registered in the system, click the <b>New Document</b> icon on the payer text field and the user will be redirected to Individual Entity (Create) window to continue creating new entity.

![alt text][create entity]
 
<br />
<br />

g.) Click the <b>Search</b> icon on the Account Title text field to open the lookup list of all the accountable forms registered in the system. Type the account title on the search bar and press Enter key. 

![alt text][search af]
 
<br />
<br />

h.) Select the accountable form and click OK. Enter the quantity and click OK.

![alt text][enter qty]
 
<br />
<br />

i.) The amount is automatically filled up derived from the data in the system. Press Enter key to commit.

![alt text][commit af]

<br />
<br />

j.) Payment Methods are <b>Cash, Check and Electronic Fund Transfer</b>.

![alt text][payment methods]

<br />
<br />

- To pay with Cash, select <b>Cash</b> on the Payment Methods or simply press <b>F9</b> key. Enter cash tendered and click OK.

	![alt text][cash tendered]

	<br />
	<br />

- To pay with Check, select <b>Check</b> on the Payment Methods or simply press <b>F10</b> key. Enter the check details and click <b>Save and Continue</b>. Mark the <b>Split Check</b> if the check amount is higher than the amount due, so that the balance can be used again for another transaction.

	![alt text][check details]

	<br />
	<br />

- To pay with Electronic Fund Transfer, select <b>EFT</b> on the Payment Methods.

	?????

	<br />
	<br />

l.) The system calculates the cash change. Prepare the cash change and click OK.

![alt text][cash change]

<br />
<br />

m.) Ensure that the following receipt number matches the actual receipt to use. Otherwise, do not proceed. Press Enter key or click OK to confirm. The print dialog box will pop up to print the receipt.

![alt text][confirm receipt]

<br />
<br />

n.) The transaction details and options are shown on the image below:

![alt text][transaction details]

- Quit - to exit the online collection.
- Add Another - to add another cash receipt transaction.
- Reprint - to print again the cash receipt. It requires a remark before reprinting.
- Print Detail - to print the details of the cash receipt without following the receipt format.
- Void - to revoke the cash receipt transaction. It requires the username, password and remarks of the approver.
- Collection Summary - ?????

<br />
<br />
<br />

II.) <b>OFFLINE</b> - this mode can be used as tellering or capturing of previous transactions. The main difference between offline and online mode is that any information can be modified even if the record is already committed. This is designed to be used in cases such as power interruption, hardware failures, etc.

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Issue Cash Receipt</b> on the <b>Collection</b> sub-menu.
<br />

![alt text][cash receipt]

<br />
<br />

b.) Select the type of transaction: Mode (should be OFFLINE), AF Type and Collection Type. Fill up the <b>Official Receipt Date</b> and click <b>Next</b>.
<br />

![alt text][offline mode]

<br />
<br />

c.) Follow the procedures of the Online Mode from <b>C to N</b>. The same process applies for the Offline Mode.

<br />
<br />
<br />

III.) <b>CAPTURE/FIELD</b> - this mode can be used to capture field collections. It allows full editing capability in order to simplify correction data for counter-checking and ensuring correctness. Once posted, capture data can no longer be edited.

<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>View Batch Captures</b> on <b>Collection</b> sub-menu.

<br />

![alt text][view batch captures]

<br />
<br />

b.) Click the <b>New Document</b> icon to issue batch capture.

<br />

![alt text][new batch capture]

<br />
<br />

c.) The user will be redirected to <b>New Batch Cash Receipt</b> tab. Input the <b>Start Series</b> based on the actual receipt used during field collection and click <b>Next</b>. Select the stub from the lookup list and click OK to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][start series]

<br />
<br />

d.) Click <b>Add</b> to start capturing the batch cash receipt.

<br />

![alt text][add batch capture]

<br />
<br />

e.) Fill up the General Information based on the actual cash receipt. Click the <b>Search</b> icon on the Account Title text field to open the lookup list of all the accountable forms registered in the system. Type the account title on the search bar and press Enter key. If the payment was made through check, click the <b>Check</b> tab and click <b>Add</b>. Enter the check details and click <b>Save and Continue</b>. Then click <b>Save</b> to proceed to the next transaction.

<br />

![alt text][batch capture]		![alt text][add check]

<br />
<br />

f.) After capturing all the cash receipts, click <b>Submit For Posting</b>. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][submit for posting]

<br />
<br />
 
g.) If all the cash receipts captured are correct, click <b>Post</b>. If there is an incorrect entry of the cash receipts, click <b>Disapprove</b> to go back to the previous page.

<br />

![alt text][post batch capture]

<br />
<br />

h.) Click <b>Submit For Online Remittance</b> to remit all cash and checks received to the liquidating officer. If there is an incorrect entry, click <b>Revert</b> to go back to the previous page.

<br />

![alt text][submit for online remittance]

<br />
<br />

i.) Click <b>Close</b> to exit Batch Capture Collection.

<br />

![alt text][close batch capture]

<br />
<br />
<br />

### 4. Remittance of Cash and Checks

<br />

<b>Job Title: COLLECTOR</b>

<b>Required Role: TREASURY.COLLECTOR</b>

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Prepare Remittance (Remit)</b> on the <b>Collection</b> sub-menu.

<br />

![alt text][remit]

<br />
<br />

b.) The receipts summary and voided receipts are displayed. Click <b>Submit for Remittance</b>. A message will pop up confirming the remittance is being submitted. Press Enter key or click OK to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][submit remittance]

<br />
<br />

c.) Click <b>Cash Breakdown</b> to input all the cash received by each currency denomination.

<br />

![alt text][cash breakdown]

<br />
<br />

d.) Enter the quantity of each currency denomination.

*Note: "Cash Remaining" should be "0.00 (zero)".*

<br />

![alt text][denomination]

<br />
<br />

e.) After assessing all the cash and checks received, click <b>Submit for Liquidation</b> to forward the remittance to the liquidation officer. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][submit liquidation]

<br />
<br />

f.) Click <b>Change Liquidating Officer</b> to choose a liquidating officer who will liquidate the remittance submitted. Select a liquidating officer from the lookup list and click OK to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][change liquidating officer]

<br />
<br />

g.) Various remittance reports are automatically generated under <b>Reports</b> button.

<br />

![alt text][remittance reports]

<br />
<br />
<br />

### 5. Liquidation of Collections

<br />

<b>Job Title: LIQUIDATING OFFICER</b>

<b>Required Role: TREASURY.LIQUIDATING_OFFICER</b>

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Prepare Collection Voucher</b> on the <b>Remittance/Deposit</b> sub-menu.

<br />

![alt text][prepare collection voucher]

<br />
<br />

b.) Select and double-click the collection remitted by the collector to view the report.

<br />

![alt text][liq view report]

<br />
<br />

c.) If the Report on Collections and Deposits is correct, click <b>Accept</b>. Otherwise, click <b>Send Back</b> to send back the remittance to the collector.

<br />

![alt text][accept remittance]

<br />
<br />

d.) Click <b>Affix Collector Signature</b> and <b>Affix Approver Signature</b> to attach the signatures on the report. Then click <b>Close</b> to exit.

<br />

![alt text][affix signature]

<br />
<br />

e.) Click <b>Submit for Liquidation</b>. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][submit liq]

<br />
<br />

f.) Click <b>Update Cash Breakdown</b> and input all the cash received by each currency denomination.

<br />

![alt text][update cash breakdown]

<br />
<br />

g.) Click <b>Post</b>. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][post]

<br />
<br />

h.) Various liquidation reports are automatically generated under <b>Reports</b> button.

<br />

![alt text][liquidation report]

<br />
<br />
<br />

### 6. Preparation of Deposit Slip

<br />

<b>Job Title: CASHIER</b>

<b>Required Role: TREASURY.CASHIER</b>

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Prepare Deposit Voucher</b> on the <b>Remittance/Deposit</b> sub-menu.

<br />

![alt text][prepare deposit voucher]

<br />
<br />

b.) Mark check the liquidated collection and click <b>Submit</b>. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][check and submit liq]

<br />
<br />

c.) A dialog box will pop up confirming the deposit voucher control no. created. Click OK to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][deposit voucher created]

<br />
<br />

d.) The user will be redirected to the <b>New Deposit Voucher</b> tab. Click <b>View Fund</b>.

<br />

![alt text][view fund]

<br />
<br />

e.) The user will be redirected to the <b>Deposit Voucher by Fund</b> tab. Click <b>Add Deposit Slip</b>.

<br />

![alt text][add dep slip]

<br />
<br />

f.) The <b>New Deposit Slip</b> window will pop up. Fill up the Bank Account and Deposit Date. Mark check the checks to be deposited under <b>Checks and Other Payments</b>. Input all the cash to be deposited by each currency denomination under <b>Cash Breakdown</b>. Then click <b>Save</b>.

<br />

![alt text][fill up dep slip]

<br />
<br />

g.) The user will be redirected again to the <b>Deposit Voucher by Fund</b> tab. The deposit slips are separated by deposit type. Mark check the deposit slip and click <b>Approve for Printing</b>. If successful, the status of the deposit slip will be <b>"APPROVED"</b>.

*Note: Do this one at a time per deposit slip.*

<br />

![alt text][approve dep slip]

<br />
<br />

h.) Mark check the approved deposit slip and click <b>Print</b>. The print dialog box will pop up to print the deposit slip. Click <b>Mark as Printed</b> if the deposit slip is successfuly printed.

*Note: Do this one at a time per deposit slip.*

<br />

![alt text][print dep slip]

<br />
<br />

i.) Mark check the printed deposit slip and click <b>Validate</b>. The <b>Validate Deposit Slip</b> dialog box will pop up. Fill up the <b>Validation Ref No</b> and <b>Validation Ref Date</b>. Then click OK.

*Note: Do this one at a time per deposit slip.*

<br />

![alt text][validate dep slip]			![alt text][validate2 dep slip]

<br />
<br />

j.) Click the <b>X</b> icon to exit the <b>Deposit Voucher by Fund</b> tab.

<br />

![alt text][exit dep voucher fund]

<br />
<br />

k.) Go back to <b>New Deposit Voucher</b> tab and click <b>Post</b> to post the deposit slip to the system. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][post dep slip]

<br />
<br />

l.) A dialog box will pop up confirming that the posting is successful. Click OK.

<br />

![alt text][successful dep slip]
