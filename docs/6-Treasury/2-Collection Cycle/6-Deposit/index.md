<b>6. Preparation of Deposit Slip</b>
---

<br />

<b>Job Title: CASHIER</b>

<b>Required Role: TREASURY.CASHIER</b>

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Prepare Deposit Voucher</b> on the <b>Remittance/Deposit</b> sub-menu.

<br />

![alt text][prepare deposit voucher]

<br />
<br />

b.) Mark check the liquidated collection and click <b>Submit</b>. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][check and submit liq]

<br />
<br />

c.) A dialog box will pop up confirming the deposit voucher control no. created. Click OK to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][deposit voucher created]

<br />
<br />

d.) The user will be redirected to the <b>New Deposit Voucher</b> tab. Click <b>View Fund</b>.

<br />

![alt text][view fund]

<br />
<br />

e.) The user will be redirected to the <b>Deposit Voucher by Fund</b> tab. Click <b>Add Deposit Slip</b>.

<br />

![alt text][add dep slip]

<br />
<br />

f.) The <b>New Deposit Slip</b> window will pop up. Fill up the Bank Account and Deposit Date. Mark check the checks to be deposited under <b>Checks and Other Payments</b>. Input all the cash to be deposited by each currency denomination under <b>Cash Breakdown</b>. Then click <b>Save</b>.

<br />

![alt text][fill up dep slip]

<br />
<br />

g.) The user will be redirected again to the <b>Deposit Voucher by Fund</b> tab. The deposit slips are separated by deposit type. Mark check the deposit slip and click <b>Approve for Printing</b>. If successful, the status of the deposit slip will be <b>"APPROVED"</b>.

*Note: Do this one at a time per deposit slip.*

<br />

![alt text][approve dep slip]

<br />
<br />

h.) Mark check the approved deposit slip and click <b>Print</b>. The print dialog box will pop up to print the deposit slip. Click <b>Mark as Printed</b> if the deposit slip is successfuly printed.

*Note: Do this one at a time per deposit slip.*

<br />

![alt text][print dep slip]

<br />
<br />

i.) Mark check the printed deposit slip and click <b>Validate</b>. The <b>Validate Deposit Slip</b> dialog box will pop up. Fill up the <b>Validation Ref No</b> and <b>Validation Ref Date</b>. Then click OK.

*Note: Do this one at a time per deposit slip.*

<br />

![alt text][validate dep slip]

<br />

![alt text][validate2 dep slip]

<br />
<br />

j.) Click the <b>X</b> icon to exit the <b>Deposit Voucher by Fund</b> tab.

<br />

![alt text][exit dep voucher fund]

<br />
<br />

k.) Go back to <b>New Deposit Voucher</b> tab and click <b>Post</b> to post the deposit slip to the system. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][post dep slip]

<br />
<br />

l.) A dialog box will pop up confirming that the posting is successful. Click OK.

<br />

![alt text][successful dep slip]

<br />
<br />
<br />
