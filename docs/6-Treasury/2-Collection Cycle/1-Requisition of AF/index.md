<b>1. Requisition of Accountable Form</b>
---

<br />

<b>Job Title: COLLECTOR</b>

<b>Required Role: TREASURY.COLLECTOR</b>

<br />
<br />

a.) From the home screen, go to <b>Treasury</b>. Click <b>Request Accountable Form</b> on the <b>Collection</b> sub-menu.
<br />

![alt text][request af]

<br />
<br />

b.) Click the <b>Search</b> icon under the item code to open the lookup list of the accountable forms. Select an Accountable Form and click OK. Or simply type the item code on the text field and click OK. The <b>title</b> and <b>unit</b> will automatically be filled up. Input the number of stub under <b>Qty</b> and press Enter key to commit.
<br />

![alt text][item code]

<br />
<br />

c.) Click the <b>Save</b> icon. A confirmation dialog box will pop up. Click "Yes" to proceed.

<br />

![alt text][save request af]

<br />
<br />

d.) The requisition of accountable form is successful with a "Req. No" for reference. Click the <b>Preview</b> icon to view the "Requisition and Issue Slip" report.

<br />

![alt text][view ris]

<br />
<br />

e.) Click the <b>Save</b> icon to save a document file of the RIS report. Click the <b>Print</b> icon to print the RIS report.

<br />

![alt text][ris report]
